What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber-fraud that targets users via sending an email or text message posing as a legitimate institution or individual to deceive users to provide sensitive information such as personal information, banking details and passwords.

How to avoid Phishing?

1. Do not click attached link in the message you received.
2. Do not give any information over the phone or in the internet.
3. Do not download any attachment in the message.
4. If you're in doubt,
click here to use our Phishing Website Detection System and to check whether the link is phishing or benign.
5. Report it.
